'''미국 신학회 '''(American Theological Society)는 [북아메리카](../Page/북아메리카.md "wikilink")에서 [1912년](../Page/1912년.md "wikilink")에 창립된 가장 오래된 전문적인 [신학회](../Page/신학회.md "wikilink")이다.  미국의 동부지역인 [프린스턴 신학교과](../Page/프린스턴_신학교.md "wikilink") 같은 곳에서 적어도 일년에 한번은 모인다. 멤버쉽은 회원의 2/3의 투표에 의해서 지명 혹은 선택이 되는데 한번에 100명만 제한된다. 미국 신학회의 목적은 다양한 신학적 연구의 증진과 서로의 관계를 개선하는 것이다.

## 관련사회

### 미국내 신학회들

  - Catholic Theological Society of America (founded in 1946; [1](https://web.archive.org/web/20180901075702/https://ctsa-online.org/))
  - The Evangelical Theological Society (founded in 1949; [website](http://www.etsjets.org/))
  - Wesleyan Theological Society (founded in 1965; [website](https://web.archive.org/web/20180823070309/http://wtsweb.org/))
  - Orthodox Theological Society in America (founded in 1966; [website](https://web.archive.org/web/20180831041936/http://www.otsamerica.org/))
  - Adventist Theological Society (probably founded in 1988; [website](https://web.archive.org/web/20180825124856/http://www.atsjats.org/))

### 지역별 신학회

  - American Theological Society Midwest Division (founded in 1927; [website](https://web.archive.org/web/20181125161646/https://www.americantheologicalsociety.org/))
  - Pacific Coast Theological Society (founded in 1939; [website](http://www.pcts.org/))
  - College Theology Society (founded in 1953; [website](https://web.archive.org/web/20181101174346/http://collegetheologysociety10.wildapricot.org/))
  - Old Life Theological Society (founded around 2004; [website](https://web.archive.org/web/20150402103624/https://www.collegetheology.org/))
  - Fox Valley Theological Society (founded around 2009 or 2010; [website](https://web.archive.org/web/20180117194240/http://fvts.net/))
  - Boston Theological Society (founding year unknown; [website](https://web.archive.org/web/20141109152921/http://www.bostontheological.org/boston_theological_society.html))
