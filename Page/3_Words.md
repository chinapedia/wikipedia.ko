## 수록곡

1.  3 Words (featuring will.i.am)
2.  [Parachute](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parachute "wikilink")
3.  Heaven (featuring will.i.am)
4.  [Fight for This Love](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fight_for_This_Love "wikilink")
5.  Rain on Me
6.  Make Me Cry
7.  Happy Hour
8.  Stand Up (featuring Taio Cruz)
9.  Don't Talk About This Love
10. Boy Like You (featuring will.i.am)
11. Heartbreaker (will.i.am featuring Cheryl Cole)
