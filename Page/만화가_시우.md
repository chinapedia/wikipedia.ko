[섬네일](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/파일:Wiki-siwoo.asia.jpg "wikilink") **만화가 시우**는 [대한민국](../Page/대한민국.md "wikilink")의 만화 작가이다.

작품은 'DEATH MASK', '하카란다', '이리안 로드' 등을 서적 출판 했고

포털 사이트 'NAVER' 에서 " 창위의 일루젼" 을 연재했다.

1인창조기업 Soha studio를 대표한다

"**SI WOO**" is a cartoonist in South Korea.

She published several works in books.

The works include 'DEATH MASK,' 'Hakaranda,' ' Irian Road,' and 'Sleeping World.'

She participated in several films and CFs as storyboard writer.

She also has a career as an artist in a game company.

As a representative work, on the Korean portal site 'NAVER'

It was a series of works called " **[The Illusion of the lance](https://comic.naver.com/webtoon/detail.nhn?titleId=26431&no=1&weekday=wed)**."

At the time of the series, the readership was 360,000.

To publish for herself, she created a one-person company.

The name is "Soha Studio".

Her homepage is " **[SIWOO.ASIA](http://www.siwoo.asia)**".


She claimed the value of the original cartoon to a Korean court for the first time in Korea.

This has been going on for seven years.
