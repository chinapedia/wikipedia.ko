**보행 훈련**은 걷는 자세와 방법의 훈련을 통해 장애를 극복하거나 치료, 예방하는 훈련이다.\[1\]

**2. Task related training**

1\) Task specific training

(1) Overground gait training

(2) Treadmill gait training

(3) Body weight supported gait training

(4) Body weight supported treadmill gait training

(5) Robot assisted gait training

2\) Task oriented gait training

(1) Muscle strengthening: closed kinetic

(2) Step up

(3) Step down

(4) Step down touch

patients must first conduct muscle training exercises.

They must repeat exercises of walking up and down the foothold, and using feet to touch the ground from the foothold.

Finally, actual walking exercises must be conducted in order to practice correct walking posture.
