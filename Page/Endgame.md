## 목록

1.  "Dialectic Chaos" – 2:25
2.  "This Day We Fight\!"– 3:27
3.  "[44 Minutes](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/44_Minutes "wikilink")" – 4:37
4.  "1,320" – 3:49
5.  "[Bite the Hand](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bite_the_Hand "wikilink")" – 4:01
6.  "Bodies" – 3:34
7.  "Endgame" – 5:56
8.  "The Hardest Part Of Letting Go...Sealed With A Kiss" – 4:41
9.  "[Head Crusher](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Head_Crusher "wikilink")" – 3:26
10. "How The Story Ends" – 4:28
11. "The Right To Go Insane" – 4:18
12. "Washington Is Next\!" (Live)" - 5:20
