**우리는 하느님과 우리 땅의 전사**(نحن جند الله جند الوطن *Nahnu Jund Allah Jund Al-watan*, We are the soldiers of God, we are the soldiers of our Homeland)는 [수단](../Page/수단.md "wikilink")의 국가이다.

랍어

  -

    نحن جند الله جند الوطن
    إن دعى داعي الفداء لم نخن
    نتحدى الموت عند المحن
    نشتري المجد بأغلى ثمن
    هذه الأرض لنا
    فليعيش سوداننا
    علماً بين الأمم
    يا بني السودان هذا رمزكـم
    يحمل العبء و يحمي أرضكم

## 아랍어 음역

  -
    Naḥnu Jundu-llāh jundul waṭan.
    Indaʿa dāʿīll-fidā′ lam nakhun.
    Nataḥaddal maut ʿind-al miḥan.
    Nashtarīl majd bi-aghlā thaman.
    Hadhihil arḍ lanā Falayaʿīsh :Sūdānunā,ʿalaman bainal Umam.
    Yā banī-s Sudān, hadhā ramzukum;
    Yaḥmalil uʿba′, wa yaḥmī arḍakum.

## 영어 해석

  -
    [We are the soldiers of God](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudanese_Armed_Forces "wikilink"), the soldiers of our homeland,
    We never fail when called for sacrifice.
    Death, we defy at adversity.
    Glory, we buy with the highest price.
    [This land](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan "wikilink") is ours, may, our Sudan, live long, an edifice among the nations,
    O [Sons of the Sudan](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudanese_people "wikilink"), this is your symbol,
    which shoulders your burden and shields your land.
