***Undisputed LP***는 [대한민국](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/대한민국 "wikilink")의 [힙합](../Page/힙합.md "wikilink") 래퍼 [Dead' P가](../Page/데드피.md "wikilink") [2004년](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/2004년 "wikilink") [6월 15일](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/6월_15일 "wikilink") 발표한 정규 앨범이다.

## 트랙

1\. Intro

  - produced by Mild Beats for Big Deal Records
  - lyric by Dead'P

2\. Undisputed (feat. Bust This, DJ Pumkin)

  - produced by Loptimist for Big Deal Records
  - lyric by Dead'P
  - scratch by Bust This for Nega Family, DJ Pumkin of Unknown DJs

3\. Mephisto (feat. Abstract'eller aka Rhyme -A-, DJ Crown)

  - produced by Loptimist for Big Deal Records
  - lyric by Dead'P, Abstract'eller for 신의의지 Records
  - scratch by DJ Crown

4\. 날개짓 (feat. Paloalto, DJ Crown)

  - produced by Primary for Big Deal Records
  - lyric by Dead'P, Paloalto for 신의의지 Records
  - scratch by DJ Crown

5\. Big Deal Anthem 2004

  - produced by Loptimist for Big Deal Records
  - lyric by Marco for mo'Real, Dynamite, Dead P, 425, Deep Flow, Shock_E, Addsp2ch
  - scratch by DJ Toon, DJ Switch

6\. It Goes Down Once Again

  - produced by Loptimist for Big Deal Records
  - lyric by Dead'P

7\. Black List (feat. Loptimist, Deep Flow)

  - produced by Loptimist for Big Deal Records
  - lyric by Dead'P, Loptimist, Deep Flow for Big Deal Records
  - scratch by Bust This for Nega Family

8\. Honesty (Loptimistic Remix) (feat. Addsp2ch)

  - produced by Loptimist for Big Deal Records
  - lyric by Dead'P, Addsp2ch for Big Deal Records

9\. C O R E A

  - produced by Mild Beats for Big Deal Records
  - lyric by Dead'P

10\. 길위의 빛 (feat. mc K)

  - produced by Loptimist for Big Deal Records
  - lyric by Dead'P, MC K for Ace Valley Crew
  - scratch by Bust This for Nega Family

14\. Hidden Track (Big Deal Anthem 2004 Remix)

  - lyric by Dead'P, Deepflow, MARCO, Dynamite, SIO, Shock-E

15\. Hidden Track (Mephisto Remix)

  - lyric by Dead'P, Rhyme -A-
  - scratch by DJ Crown
