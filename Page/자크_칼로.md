## 주된 작품들

[섬네일](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/파일:Jacques_Callot_-_La_foire_d'Impruneta,_1620.jpg "wikilink")

  - A large series depicting *[commedia dell'arte](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/콤메디아_델라르테 "wikilink")* figures called *Balli di Sfessania*, in a simple, [caricature](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/캐리커처 "wikilink")-like style, from his years in [Florence](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/피렌체 "wikilink").
  - Series on the Lives of Christ and Mary.
  - Series on the story of the Prodigal Son.
  - *The Giant Tifeo beneath Mount Ischia* (1617).
  - *The Fair at [Impruneta](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/임프루네타 "wikilink")* (1620).
  - *The Fair at Gondreville* (1624).
  - *The Temptation of St Anthony* (1635, Fitzwillaim Museum).[*Temptation of Saint Anthony*](http://www.fitzmuseum.cam.ac.uk/dept/pdp/prints/highlights/63.html)
