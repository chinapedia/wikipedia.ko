**이 순 민**(李 淳 敏, SOON MIN LEE [1972년](../Page/1972년.md "wikilink") [1월 24일](../Page/1월_24일.md "wikilink") \~ )은 [대한민국](../Page/대한민국.md "wikilink")의 [기업인](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/기업인 "wikilink")이자 [주식회사베스트건축](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/주식회사베스트건축 "wikilink") 대표이사이다.

## EDUCATION BACKGROUND

  - A [PhD](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/PhD "wikilink") in Engineering, Joongbu University: The Graduate School, Construction Convergence Management
  - Master of Engineering, Construction Management and Technology Dankook University: The Graduate School of Management, Architectural Systems Management
  - Bachelor of Engineering, Nam Seoul University, Architecture
