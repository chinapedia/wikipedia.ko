[thumb](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/파일:Teddy_Hart_17.png "wikilink") **테디 하트**(Teddy Hart, [1980년](../Page/1980년.md "wikilink") [2월 2일](../Page/2월_2일.md "wikilink") \~ )는 본명은 **에드워드 엘스워스 애니스**(Edward Ellsworth Annis)다. [캐나다](../Page/캐나다.md "wikilink")의 남자 프로레슬링선수다. 키는 186cm(6ft 1in) 몸무게는 95kg(209lb)이다. 1995년 프로레슬링 입문으로 밝혀진다.

## Professional wrestling highlights

  - **Finishing moves**
      - Diving headbutt drop
      - *Hart Attack* (Shooting star press)
      - *Hart Attack 2.0* (Shooting star elbow drop)
      - *Hart Destroyer* (Double underhook sunset flip piledriver)
      - *Open Hart Surgery* (Corkscrew senton bomb)
      - *Triple Bypass* (Shooting star leg drop)
  - **Signature moves**
      - Belly to back suplex bulldog
      - Cradle brainbuster
      - Diving corkscrew elbow drop
      - Gory special reverse STO
      - Modified fujiwara armbar
      - Multiples DDT variations
          - Diving tornado
          - Cradie
          - Jumping inverted
          - Spike, sometimes in a Springboard
          - Spinning double underhook
          - Springboard tornado
      - Multiples leg drop variations
          - 450°
          - Diving corkscrew
      - Multiples powerbomb variations
          - *Hart Rate* (Release belly to back suplex flipped into a)
          - *Stu Hart Special* (Sitout gory special)
      - Running front kick
      - Scoop cutter
      - Shooting star seated senton
      - Sitout fireman's carry facebuster
      - Sitout gory special slam
      - Springboard moonsault
