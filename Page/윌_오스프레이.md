[thumb](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/파일:Will_Ospreay_2016.jpg "wikilink") **윌 오스프레이**(Will Ospreay, [1993년](../Page/1993년.md "wikilink") [5월 7일](../Page/5월_7일.md "wikilink")\[1\] \~ )는 본명은 **찰리 윌리엄스**(Charlie Williams)다.\[2\] [영국](../Page/영국.md "wikilink")의 [프로레슬링](../Page/프로레슬링.md "wikilink")선수다. 키는 186cm 몸무게는 97kg의 나간다. 2012년 프로레슬링 입문으로 밝혀졌다.

## Professional wrestling highlights

  - **Finishing moves**
      - 630° Senton
      - Corkscrew senton bomb
      - Corkscrew shooting star press
      - *Oscutter* (Springboard cutter)
  - **Signature moves**
      - *Air Assassin* (Imploding 450° splash)
      - *Burning Star Press* (Shooting star press to an opponent hanging in the top rope)
      - Corkscrew stunner, as a reversal to a suplex; adopted from Dragon Kid
      - *Essex Destroyer* (Modified DDT)
      - *Iron Octopus* (Modified octopus hold)
      - Jumping shooting star press
      - Multiple elbow smash variations
          - *Hidden Blade* (Spinning)
          - *Pip Pip Cheerio* (Springboard)
      - Multiple kick variations
          - Running corkscrew high
          - Handspring enzuigiri
          - *Robinson Special* (Spinning hook)
          - Running hook
          - *Silent Whisper* (Running corkscrew scissors)
      - *Rainham Maker* (Standing moonsault side slam); adopted from Kazuchika Okada
      - Running corkscrew senton bomb
      - *Sasuke Special* / *Space Tiger Drop* (Cartwheel over the top rope suicide corkscrew moonsault)
      - Spinning high knee; used as a finisher in WCPW/DW
