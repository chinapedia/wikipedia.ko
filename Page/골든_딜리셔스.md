**골든 딜리셔스**(Golden Delicious)는 노란 빛을 내지만 [레드 딜리셔스](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/레드_딜리셔스 "wikilink") 사과와는 밀접한 관련은 없는 [사과](../Page/사과.md "wikilink")의 [재배품종](../Page/재배품종.md "wikilink")이다.\[1\] [미국 사과 협회](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/미국_사과_협회 "wikilink") 웹사이트에 따르면 미국 내 15개의 가장 대중적인 사과 재배품종 가운데 하나이다.\[2\]

## 파생 재배품종

  - [Akita Gold](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Akita_Gold "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Fuji)\[3\]
  - [Arlet](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Arlet_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Idared)
  - [Bohemia](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Bohemia_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Lord Lambourne × Golden Delicious)
  - [Candel](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Cadel_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Jonathan × Golden Delicious)
  - [Cameo](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Cameo_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Red Delicious)
  - [Caudle](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Caudle_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Red Delicious)
  - [Champion](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Champion_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Cox Orange)
  - [Chantecler](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Chantecler_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Reinette Clochard)
  - [Cripps Pink](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Cripps_Pink "wikilink") (marketed as Pink Lady; Golden Delicious × [:en:Lady Williams (apple)](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Lady_Williams_\(apple\) "wikilink"))\[4\]
  - [Delbarestivale](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Delbarestivale "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Stark Jonagrimes)
  - [Elstar](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Elstar "wikilink") (Ingrid Marie × Golden Delicious)
  - [Firm Gold](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Firm_Gold "wikilink") (Starkspur Golden Delicious, U.S. PP 2024 × Starkrimson Red Delicious, U.S. PP 1565)\[5\]
  - [Gala](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Gala_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Kidds Orange × Golden Delicious)
  - [Ginger Gold](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Ginger_Gold "wikilink") (Albemarle Pippin × Golden Delicious)
  - [Goldspur](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Goldspur "wikilink") a Golden Delicious-like cultivar from Holland which is spur bearing
  - [Iduna](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Iduna_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Glockenapfel)
  - [Jonagold](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Jonagold "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Jonathan)
  - [Lucky Rose Golden](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Lucky_Rose_Golden "wikilink") A patented Golden Delicious mutant\[6\]
  - [Pinova](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Pinova "wikilink") ([Clivia](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Clivia_\(apple\) "wikilink") × Golden Delicious)
  - [Maigold](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Maigold_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Fraurotacher × Golden Delicious)
  - [Mutsu](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Mutsu_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Indo apple × Golden Delicious)
  - [Opal](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Opal_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Topaz × Golden Delicious)
  - [Rubinette](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Rubinette_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Cox Orange)
  - [Sekai Ichi](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Sekai_Ichi "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Red Delicious)
  - [Spigold](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Spigold "wikilink") (Northern Spy x Golden Delicious)
  - [Sundowner](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Sundowner_\(apple\) "wikilink") (Golden Delicious × Lady Williams)
  - [Tentation delblush](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Tentation "wikilink") (Grifer (Blushing Golden) × Golden Delicious)
