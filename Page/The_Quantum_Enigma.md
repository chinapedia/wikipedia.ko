## Personnel

### Line-up (Band members)

  - Mark Jansen: Rhythm Guitars, Death Grunts & Screams
  - Coen Janssen: Keyboards, Piano & Synthesizer
  - Simone Simons: Lead Vocals, Soprano
  - Arien Van Weesenbeek: Drums, Grunts & Spoken Words
  - Isaac Delahaye: Lead/Rhythm Guitars & Backing Vocals
  - Rob Van Der Loo: Bass Guitars.

### Guest (Additional lineup)

  - Marcela Bovio (Stream Of Passion): Backing Vocals.

### Production staff (Miscellaneous staff)

  - Joost van den Broek: Edit, Engineer & Produced
  - Maarten de Peijper, Jos Driessen, Darius van Helfteren, Jacob Hansen: Engineer, Edit, Mastering & Mixed
  - Tonnie Simons, Stefan Heilemann: Art Directed, Artwork, Cover art, Illustrator
  - Recording Studio: Sandlane Recording Facilities, Rijen.
