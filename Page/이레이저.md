**이레이저**(Erasure)는 잉글랜드의 [신스팝](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/신스팝 "wikilink") 듀오이다.

## 음반 목록

  - *Wonderland* (1986)
  - *The Circus* (1987)
  - *The Innocents* (1988)
  - *Wild\!* (1989)
  - *Chorus* (1991)
  - *I Say I Say I Say* (1994)
  - *Erasure* (1995)
  - *Cowboy* (1997)
  - *Loveboat* (2000)
  - *Other People's Songs* (2003)
  - *Nightbird* (2005)
  - *Union Street* (2006)
  - *Light at the End of the World* (2007)
  - *Tomorrow's World* (2011)
  - *Snow Globe* (2013)
  - *The Violet Flame* (2014)
  - *World Be Gone* (2017)
