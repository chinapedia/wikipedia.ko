**델마도마뱀붙이속**(*Delma*)은 [뱀붙이도마뱀과](../Page/뱀붙이도마뱀과.md "wikilink")에 속한 [도마뱀](../Page/도마뱀.md "wikilink")의 일[속이다](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/속_\(생물학\) "wikilink"). 22 개의 유효한 종을 포함하며 모두 [오스트레일리아](../Page/오스트레일리아.md "wikilink")에 [고유하다](../Page/고유성.md "wikilink").

## 하위

후술할 델마도마뱀붙이속에 속한 종의 목록은 [파충류 데이터베이스](http://reptile-database.reptarium.cz/)에 의거하였다:

  - *[Delma australis](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_australis "wikilink")* <small>[Kluge](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arnold_G._Kluge "wikilink"), 1974</small> - marble-faced delma
  - *[Delma borea](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_borea "wikilink")* <small>Kluge, 1974</small> - rusty-topped delma
  - *[Delma butleri](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_butleri "wikilink")* <small>[Storr](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glen_Milton_Storr "wikilink"), 1987</small> - unbanded delma, Butler's legless lizard, spinifex snake-lizard
  - *[Delma concinna](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_concinna "wikilink")* <small>(Kluge, 1974)</small> - javelin lizard
  - *[Delma desmosa](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_desmosa "wikilink")* <small>[Maryan](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brad_Maryan "wikilink"), [Aplin](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kenneth_Peter_Aplin "wikilink") & [Adams](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mark_Adams_\(herpetologist\) "wikilink"), 2007</small> - desert delma
  - *[Delma elegans](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_elegans "wikilink")* <small>Kluge, 1974</small> - Pilbara delma
  - *[Delma fraseri](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_fraseri "wikilink")* <small>[Gray](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Edward_Gray "wikilink"), 1831</small> - Fraser's delma
  - *[Delma grayii](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_grayii "wikilink")* <small>[A. Smith](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andrew_Smith_\(zoologist\) "wikilink"), 1849</small> - side-barred delma, Gray's legless lizard
  - *[Delma hebesa](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_hebesa "wikilink")* <small>Maryan, Brennan, Adams, & Aplin, 2015</small> - heath delma
  - *[Delma impar](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/줄무늬민다리도마뱀 "wikilink")* <small>([Fischer](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:fr:Johann_Gustav_Fischer "wikilink"), 1882)</small> - striped legless lizard
  - *[Delma inornata](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_inornata "wikilink")* <small>Kluge, 1974</small> - patternless delma
  - *[Delma labialis](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_labialis "wikilink")* <small>[Shea](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glen_Michael_Shea "wikilink"), 1987</small> - striped-tailed delma, single-striped delma
  - *[Delma mitella](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_mitella "wikilink")* <small>Shea, 1987</small> - Atherton delma
  - *[Delma molleri](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/민무늬델마도마뱀 "wikilink")* <small>[Lütken](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christian_Frederik_Lütken "wikilink"), 1863</small> - Gulfs delma, olive legless lizard, patternless delma
  - *[Delma nasuta](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_nasuta "wikilink")* <small>Kluge, 1974</small> - sharp-snouted delma, sharp-snouted legless lizard
  - *[Delma pax](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_pax "wikilink")* <small>Kluge, 1974</small> - peace delma
  - *[Delma petersoni](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_petersoni "wikilink")* <small>Shea, 1991</small> - painted delma
  - *[Delma plebeia](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_plebeia "wikilink")* <small>[De Vis](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Walter_De_Vis "wikilink"), 1888</small> - leaden delma
  - *[Delma tealei](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_tealei "wikilink")* <small>Maryan, Aplin, & Adams, 2007</small> - north west Cape delma
  - *[Delma tincta](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/놀란델마도마뱀 "wikilink")* <small>De Vis, 1888</small> - excitable delma
  - *[Delma torquata](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delma_torquata "wikilink")* <small>Kluge, 1974</small> - collared delma, adorned delma

*[Nota bene](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nota_bene "wikilink")*: A [binomial authority](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Binomial_nomenclature "wikilink") in parentheses indicates that the species was originally described in a genus other than *Delma*.
