[섬네일](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/파일:Vaccination-polio-india.jpg "wikilink") **예방 접종**(豫防接種, )은 개인의 면역계를 자극하여 [병원체](../Page/병원체.md "wikilink")에 대한 [적응 면역성을](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/후천_면역 "wikilink") 발달시킬 목적으로 [항원](../Page/항원.md "wikilink") 물질 ([백신](../Page/백신.md "wikilink"))을 주사하는 일을 가리킨다.

예방 접종의 효력은 이를테면 [인플루엔자 백신](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/인플루엔자_백신 "wikilink")\[1\], [HPV 백신](../Page/HPV_백신.md "wikilink")\[2\], [수두 백신](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/수두_백신 "wikilink")\[3\] 등을 통해 널리 연구되고 입증되어 왔다. 예방 접종은 [감염병](../Page/감염병.md "wikilink")을 예방하는 가장 효율적인 방법이다.<ref>\* United States Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (2011). [*A CDC framework for preventing infectious diseases.*](http://www.cdc.gov/oid/docs/ID-Framework.pdf) Accessed 11 September 2012. “Vaccines are our most effective and cost-saving tools for disease prevention, preventing untold suffering and saving tens of thousands of lives and billions of dollars in healthcare costs each year.”

  - American Medical Association (2000). [*Vaccines and infectious diseases: putting risk into perspective.*](http://www.immunizationinfo.org/es/pressroom/2000-06-01/vaccines-and-infectious-diseases-putting-risk-perspective)  Accessed 11 September 2012. “Vaccines are the most effective public health tool ever created.”
  - Public Health Agency of Canada. [*Vaccine-preventable diseases.*](http://www.phac-aspc.gc.ca/im/vpd-mev/index-eng.php) Accessed 11 September 2012. “Vaccines still provide the most effective, longest-lasting method of preventing infectious diseases in all age groups.”
  - United States National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases (NIAID). [*NIAID Biodefense Research Agenda for Category B and C Priority Pathogens.*](http://virtualbiosecuritycenter.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Library-NIAID-Biodefense-Research-Agenda-for-Category-B-and-C-Priority-Pathogens.pdf)  Accessed 11 September 2012. “Vaccines are the most effective method of protecting the public against infectious diseases.”</ref> 예방 접종에 의한 광범위한 면역은 [천연두](../Page/천연두.md "wikilink")의 박멸과 [소아마비](../Page/소아마비.md "wikilink"), [홍역](../Page/홍역.md "wikilink"), [파상풍](../Page/파상풍.md "wikilink") 등의 질병을 제한하는 효과를 보였다. [세계 보건 기구](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/세계_보건_기구 "wikilink")(WHO)는 현재 허가된 백신이 25 종류의 감염병의 예방과 제어에 기여한다는 보고서를 작성하기도 했다.
