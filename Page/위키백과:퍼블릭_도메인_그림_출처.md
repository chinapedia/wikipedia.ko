## 위키미디어 재단이 운영하는 사이트

[Commons-logo-en.svg](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Commons-logo-en.svg "fig:Commons-logo-en.svg")\]\] [위키미디어 공용에는](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Commons:Main_page "wikilink") 350만개 이상의 파일들이 있습니다. 위키미디어 공용의 사진들은 모두 위키백과 한국어판에서 사용할 수 있습니다.

## 역사

### 역사적인 사진들

  - <http://lcweb2.loc.gov/ammem/ammemhome.html> Library of Congress American Memory site (Check copyright information for the separate items before deciding to use them\!)
  - <http://teachpol.tcnj.edu/amer_pol_hist/> - public domain images of American Political History
  - <http://www.exclassics.com/newgate/ngillus.htm> Images from [The Newgate Calendar](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Newgate_Calendar "wikilink"). Mostly crimes and criminals from the 18th century. Also other works at <http://www.exclassics.com>
  - <http://www.fromoldbooks.org/> Collection images scanned from various old books that are now in the public domain. Searchable. Used to be under <http://www.holoweb.net/~liam/>
  - <http://www.john-leech-archive.org.uk/> John Leech sketch archives 1841 until 1864 from Punch magazine The page states they are long out of copyright and that they are believed to be public domain.
  - <http://beinecke.library.yale.edu/dl_crosscollex/> Yale University's Beinecke Rare Book & Manuscript Library's online collection of digital images. Most will be {PD-art}. 90,000 images from rare books and manuscripts, search by keyword.
  - <http://digitalgallery.nypl.org> New York Public Library. Over 500,000 images scanned from books. Including illuminated manuscripts, historical maps, vintage posters, rare prints and photographs, illustrated books, printed ephemera, and more. There is a per-image usage fee even for public domain images.
  - <http://www.oldbookillustrations.com/> Images scanned from old books. States that images on the site are the works of artists who "...have been dead for over seventy years, which makes them part of the public domain in many countries". Searchable by keywords.
  - [America As It Was](http://patsabin.com/VintagePostcards/) - a huge resource for vintage postcards in the U.S., organized by state. Any postcard first published in the U.S. before 1978 without an explicit copyright notice is PD. Lots of photos, aerial views, and maps of many U.S. locations.
  - ["The Secret Museum of Mankind"](http://ian.macky.net/secretmuseum/) - collection of anthropological photographs published in a 1935 book without copyright. Scanned and released under a CC-NC license, but images should be public domain, at least in the US, since they are faithful reproductions of PD images. (Warning: strong 1930's racist P.O.V.)

### 특정한 시대에 대한 사진들

  - [World War II Poster Collection](http://www.library.northwestern.edu/govpub/collections/wwii-posters/) 미 연방 기관에 의해 발행된 300개 넘는 포스터들
  - [Photos of the Great War](http://www.gwpda.org/photos/greatwar.htm) [제1차 세계 대전의](../Page/제1차_세계_대전.md "wikilink") 많은 이미지들, 퍼블릭 도메인 이미지를 스캔함. 약간의 주의가 필요합니다. GFDL 라이선스가 적용되었을 수도 있습니다. (requires attribution and link, as does GFDL, requests e-mail notice of usage).
  - [The Heritage of the Great War](http://greatwar.nl/kleur/kleur.html) 약간의 제 1차 세계 대전의 컬러 사진 (likely all PD, since the effort taken to scan/reproduce them probably fails to qualify for being a derivative work).
  - [World's Armed Forces Forum](http://www.network54.com/Forum/thread?forumid=211833&messageid=1088370788&lp=1088%20381193) (gallery for images taken from [the French Ministry of Culture's website](http://www.culture.gouv.fr/)) hosts more colour images of World War I (likely all PD, since the effort taken to scan/reproduce them probably fails to qualify for being a derivative work).
  - <http://www.historyplace.com/unitedstates/childlabor/> 아동 노동 in America 1908 - 1912 by [Lewis W. Hine](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lewis_W._Hine "wikilink")
  - [Franklin D. Roosevelt Presidential Library and Museum](http://www.fdrlibrary.marist.edu/photos.html) Franklin 과 Eleanor Roosevelt의 수천 개의 저작권 없는 온라인 사진들, [대공황](../Page/대공황.md "wikilink"), [뉴딜 정책과](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/뉴딜_정책 "wikilink") [제2차 세계 대전](../Page/제2차_세계_대전.md "wikilink")
