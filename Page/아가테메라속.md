**아가테메라속**(*Agathemera*)은 [대벌레](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/대벌레목 "wikilink") 속의 하나이다. **아가테메라아목**(Agathemerodea)의 유일속이다.

## 종

  - **아가테메라아속** (*Agathemera*)
      - *Agathemera claraziana* <small>(Saussure, 1868)</small>
      - *Agathemera crassa* <small>(Blanchard, 1851)</small>
      - *Agathemera elegans* <small>(Philippi, 1863)</small>
      - *Agathemera grylloidea* <small>(Westwood, 1859)</small>
      - *Agathemera luteola* <small>Camousseight, 2006</small>
      - *Agathemera maculafulgens* <small>Camousseight, 1995</small>
      - *Agathemera mesoauriculae* <small>Camousseight, 1995</small>
      - *Agathemera millepunctata* <small>Redtenbacher, 1906 </small>
