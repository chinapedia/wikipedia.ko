**마붑 알엄 Mahbub Alam**, 은 **이마붑**(Mahbub Lee)으로도 알려진 [방글라데시](../Page/방글라데시.md "wikilink")의 배우이자 감독이다. [1999년](../Page/1999년.md "wikilink")에 [대한민국](../Page/대한민국.md "wikilink")에 입국하였으며 그 뒤에 이주노동자 권익 향상을 위한 활동에 힘쓰고 있다. 이주민 다룬 10편의 영화에서 주연 및 조연으로 출연하였다.

2011년 4월 대한민국으로 귀화했다.\[1\]

Born in Bangladesh, Mahbub Lee came to South Korea in 1999 as a migrant worker. While witnessing injustice and discrimination against migrant workers, he joined labor movements and later engaged in media activism. He took part in a number of film projects as both a director and an actor (The Deported (2007), Bandobi (2008), The Returnee (2009), City of Crane (2010), Love in Korea (2010), among others). He is also an author of his autobiography, I am an Earthian (2010). He was director of Asia Media Culture Factory of which main activities include founding a migrant art center, Free port, and organizing Seoul Migrant Art Festival. Now he is a Movie Distributor in M\&M International.
