**국제사법위원회** 또는 사법통일국제연구소()는 독립된 국제기구로 [국제 사법의](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/국제_사법 "wikilink") 통일과 일관성을 높일 목적으로 설립된 [단체](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/단체 "wikilink")이다. 특히 국가간 [상법](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/상법 "wikilink")의 표준화에 힘쓰고 있으며 각종 [협약](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/협약 "wikilink")및 [조약](../Page/조약.md "wikilink")을 작성하고 있다. [1926년](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/1926년 "wikilink") [국제연맹](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/국제연맹 "wikilink")의 하부조직으로 설립되었으며 현재 [로마](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/로마 "wikilink")에 위치해 있다.

## 주요 업적

연구소가 1964년에 제정하여 확정{{.cw}}채택한 〈[국제물품매매에 관한 통일법](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/국제물품매매에_관한_통일법 "wikilink")〉(A Convention relating to a Uniform Law for the International Sale of Goods, 1964 ; 이하 ‘ULIS’라 한다)\[1\]과 〈국제물품매매계약의 성립에 관한 통일법〉(A Convention relating to a Uniform Law on the Formation of Contracts for the International Sale of Goods, 1964 ; 이하 ‘ULF’라 한다)\[2\]은 [국제 물품 매매 계약에 관한 국제 연합 협약의](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/국제_물품_매매_계약에_관한_국제_연합_협약 "wikilink") 기초자료라 할 수 있다.\[3\] 그 밖에도 다음과 같은 조약 및 협약체결에 기여하였다.

  - [International Convention on Travel Contracts (Brussels, 1970)](https://web.archive.org/web/20071231231159/http://www.unidroit.org/english/conventions/c-trav.htm)
  - [Convention providing a Uniform Law on the Form of an International Will (Washington, D.C., 1973)](https://web.archive.org/web/20080207154649/http://www.unidroit.org/english/conventions/1973wills/main.htm)
  - [Convention on Agency in the International Sale of Goods (Geneva, 1983)](https://web.archive.org/web/20071212023835/http://www.unidroit.org/english/conventions/1983agency/main.htm)
  - [UNIDROIT Convention on International Financial Leasing (Ottawa, 1988)](https://web.archive.org/web/20080110192900/http://www.unidroit.org/english/conventions/1988leasing/main.htm)
  - [UNIDROIT Convention on International Factoring (Ottawa, 1988)](https://web.archive.org/web/20080105104427/http://www.unidroit.org/english/conventions/1988factoring/main.htm)
  - [UNIDROIT Convention on Stolen or Illegally Exported Cultural Objects (Rome, 1995)](https://web.archive.org/web/20120622180434/http://www.unidroit.org/english/conventions/1995culturalproperty/main.htm)
  - [Convention on International Interests in Mobile Equipment (Cape Town, 2001)](https://web.archive.org/web/20071226205937/http://www.unidroit.org/english/conventions/mobile-equipment/main.htm)
  - [Protocol to the Convention on International Interests in Mobile Equipment on Matters Specific to Aircraft Equipment (Cape Town, 2001)](https://web.archive.org/web/20071226205937/http://www.unidroit.org/english/conventions/mobile-equipment/main.htm#NR1)
  - [The Guide to International Master Franchise Arrangements (1998)](https://web.archive.org/web/20071231183535/http://www.unidroit.org/english/guides/main.htm)
  - [The Model Franchise Disclosure Law (2002)](https://web.archive.org/web/20071224051722/http://www.unidroit.org/english/modellaws/2002franchise/main.htm)
  - [Principles of International Commercial Contracts](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Principles_of_International_Commercial_Contracts "wikilink") ([(외부 링크)the Unidroit Principles of International Commercial Contracts](https://archive.is/20130416031803/http://www.unidroit.org/english/principles/contracts/main.htm))
