## 페이지뷰 도구

  - [Pageviews](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:pageviews "wikilink") ([FAQ](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:pageviews/faq "wikilink") • [URL structure](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:pageviews/url_structure "wikilink")) – The main Pageviews tool allows you to analyze pageviews of up to 10 pages during a given time frame. You can filter statistics based on the [platform](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:Computing_platform "wikilink") and [user agent](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:User_agent "wikilink"). If you want to view data on more pages, consider using [Massviews](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/#Massviews "wikilink").
  - [Langviews](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:langviews "wikilink") ([FAQ](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:langviews/faq "wikilink") • [URL structure](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:langviews/url_structure "wikilink")) – Langviews lets you see page views statistics for a given article across all languages. Supported projects are Wikipedia, Wikivoyage, Wikibooks, Wikinews, Wikiquote, Wikisource, and Wikiversity. You can filter statistics based on the [platform](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:Computing_platform "wikilink") and [user agent](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:User_agent "wikilink").
  - \[//tools.wmflabs.org/topviews?project=ko.wikipedia.org Topviews\] ([FAQ](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:topviews/faq "wikilink") • [URL structure](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:topviews/url_structure "wikilink")) – Topviews shows you the most viewed pages of a given project. The data can be filtered by date range and [platform](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:Computing_platform "wikilink"). More information on this tool can be found [below](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/#Topviews "wikilink").
  - [Siteviews](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:siteviews "wikilink") ([FAQ](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:siteviews/faq "wikilink") • [URL structure](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:siteviews/url_structure "wikilink")) – Siteviews allows you to view pageviews or unique devices across *all pages* of a given project. You can filter statistics based on the [platform](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:Computing_platform "wikilink") and [user agent](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:User_agent "wikilink"), and compare up to 10 different projects. Additionally, you can use the "Metric" option to show [unique devices](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:Unique_user "wikilink") instead of raw pageviews.
  - [Massviews](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:massviews "wikilink") ([FAQ](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:massviews/faq "wikilink") • [URL structure](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:massviews/url_structure "wikilink")) – Massviews allows you to import a list of pages and analyze their page views. For performance reasons, you can only import up to 5,000 pages. Statistics can be filtered based on the [platform](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:Computing_platform "wikilink") and [user agent](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:User_agent "wikilink"). More information on this tool can be found [below](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/#Massviews "wikilink").
  - [Redirect Views](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:redirectviews "wikilink") ([FAQ](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:redirectviews/faq "wikilink") • [URL structure](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:redirectviews/url_structure "wikilink")) – Redirect Views lets you view pageviews stats of a given page and all of its [redirects](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:Redirect "wikilink"). This is useful because when redirects are viewed, a pageview is not registered for the target page. This tool can also suggest which redirects are the most popular. Statistics can be filtered by [platform](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:Computing_platform "wikilink") and [user agent](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:User_agent "wikilink").
  - [Userviews](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:userviews "wikilink") ([FAQ](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:userviews/faq "wikilink") • [URL structure](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/toollabs:userviews/url_structure "wikilink")) – Userviews lets you view pageviews stats of all pages created by the given user. Statistics can be filtered [platform](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:Computing_platform "wikilink") and [user agent](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/W:User_agent "wikilink"). Note that the tool may be slow or in rare cases become unresponsive when querying for users with a very large edit count.
