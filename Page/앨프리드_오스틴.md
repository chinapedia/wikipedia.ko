**앨프리드 오스틴** <small>DL</small> (Alfred Austin, 1835년 5월 30일 - 1913년 6월 2일)은 1896년 [테니슨](../Page/앨프리드_테니슨.md "wikilink") 사후 [계관시인](../Page/계관시인.md "wikilink")으로 임명된 영국의 시인이다. 그의 가장 유명한 작품은 자연을 찬미하는 산문 전원시이다.

<td><p>Now upon English soil I soon shall stand,</p>
<p>Homeward from climes that fancy deems more fair;</p>
<p>And well I know that there will greet me there</p>
<p>No soft foam fawning upon smiling strand,</p>
<p>No scent of orange-groves, no zephyrs bland;</p>
<p>But Amazonian March, with breast half bare</p>
<p>And sleety arrows whistling through the air,</p>
<p>Will be my welcome from that burly land.</p>
<p>Yet he who boasts his birth-place yonder lies</p>
<p>Owns in his heart a mood akin to scorn</p>
<p>For sensuous slopes that bask 'neath Southern skies,</p>
<p>Teeming with wine and prodigal of corn,</p>
<p>And, gazing through the mist with misty eyes,</p>
