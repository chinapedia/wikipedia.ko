### 듀얼 코어 데스크톱 프로세서

#### 코어 2 듀오

##### "Conroe" (65 nm, 800 MT/s)

Note: The [M0 and G0 Steppings](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/인텔_코어_\(마이크로아키텍처\) "wikilink") have better optimizations to lower idle power consumption from 12W to 8W.

##### "Conroe" (65 nm, 1066 MT/s)

Note: of the E6000 series processors, only models E6550, E6750, and E6850 support Intel's [TXT](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Trusted_Execution_Technology "wikilink").\[1\]

Note: The [L2 Stepping](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/인텔_코어_\(마이크로아키텍처\) "wikilink") as well as the models with sSpec SL9ZL, SL9ZF, SLA4U, SLA4T have better optimizations to lower the idle power consumption from 22W to 12W.\[2\]

Note: The [M0 and G0 Steppings](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/인텔_코어_\(마이크로아키텍처\) "wikilink") have better optimizations to lower idle power consumption from 12W to 8W.

##### "Conroe" (65 nm, 1333 MT/s)

Note: of the E6000 series processors, only models E6550, E6750, and E6850 support Intel's [TXT](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Trusted_Execution_Technology "wikilink").\[3\]

Note: The [L2 Stepping](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/인텔_코어_\(마이크로아키텍처\) "wikilink") as well as the models with sSpec SL9ZL, SL9ZF, SLA4U, SLA4T have better optimizations to lower the idle power consumption from 22W to 12W.\[4\]

Note: The [M0 and G0 Steppings](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/인텔_코어_\(마이크로아키텍처\) "wikilink") have better optimizations to lower idle power consumption from 12W to 8W.

##### "Conroe-CL" (65 nm, 1066 MT/s)

##### "Wolfdale-3M" (45 nm, 1066 MT/s)

##### "Wolfdale" (45 nm, 1333 MT/s)
