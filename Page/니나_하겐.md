**니나 하겐**(Nina Hagen, [1955년](../Page/1955년.md "wikilink") [3월 11일](../Page/3월_11일.md "wikilink") \~ )은 [독일](../Page/독일.md "wikilink")의 [펑크록](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/펑크록 "wikilink") [가수](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/가수 "wikilink"), 배우이다.

## 음반 목록

  - *Nina Hagen Band* (1978)
  - *Unbehagen* (1979)
  - *NunSexMonkRock* (1982)
  - *Fearless / Angstlos* (1983)
  - *Nina Hagen in Ekstasy / In Ekstase* (1985)
  - *Nina Hagen* (1989)
  - *Street* (1991)
  - *Revolution Ballroom* (1993)
  - *FreuD euch / BeeHappy* (1995)
  - *Om Namah Shivay* (1999)
  - *Return of the Mother* (2000)
  - *Big Band Explosion* (2003)
  - *Irgendwo auf der Welt* (2006)
  - *Personal Jesus* (2010)
  - *Volksbeat* (2011)
