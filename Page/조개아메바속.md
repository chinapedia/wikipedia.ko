  - *Vannella aberdonica*

  - *Vannella ebro*
  - *Vannella epipetala*
  - *Vannella lata*
  - 꼬리조개아메바 (*Vannella mira*)
  - *Vannella miroides*
  - *Vannella persistens*
  - *Vannella platypodia*
  - *Vannella septentrionalis*
  - *Vannella simplex*
