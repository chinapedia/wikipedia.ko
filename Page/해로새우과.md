  - *Globospongicola* <small>Komai & Saito, 2006</small>

  - *Microprosthema* <small>Stimpson, 1860</small>
  - *Paraspongicola* <small>De Saint Laurent & Cléva, 1981</small>
  - 해로새우속 (*Spongicola*) <small>De Haan, 1844</small>

  - *Spongiocaris* <small>Bruce & Baba, 1973</small>
