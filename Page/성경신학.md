**성경신학**(聖經神學, Biblical theology)또는 **성서신학**(聖書神學)은 [성경](../Page/성경.md "wikilink")의 [교리](../Page/교리.md "wikilink")적인 접근보다는 [구속](../Page/구속.md "wikilink")계시의 점진적인 발전을 연구하는 [기독교](../Page/기독교.md "wikilink") 신학 분야이다. [요한 필립 게블러가](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/요한_필립_게블러 "wikilink")\[1\] 시작한 이후에 이 성경신학은 근대에 [프린스턴 신학교의](../Page/프린스턴_신학교.md "wikilink") [게할더스 보스와](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/게할더스_보스 "wikilink") 같은 [신학자](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/신학자 "wikilink")들에 의해 크게 발전하였다. [웨스트민스터 신학교의](../Page/웨스트민스터_신학교.md "wikilink") [에드워드 J. 영과](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/에드워드_J._영 "wikilink") [리차드 게핀과](../Page/리차드_게핀.md "wikilink") 같은 학자들에 의해서 널리 보급되었다.

## 외부 링크

  - [The Idea of Biblical Theology as a Science and as a Theological Discipline](https://web.archive.org/web/20060619101629/http://homepage.mac.com/shanerosenthal/reformationink/gvbiblical.htm) - classic definition of Biblical theology by Geerhardus Vos (1894)
  - [BiblicalTheology.org](http://www.biblicaltheology.org/) - writings of Geerhardus Vos, who is sometimes called "the father of [Reformed](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Reformed_churches "wikilink") Biblical Theology"
  - [*Kerux: The Journal of Northwest Theological Seminary*](http://www.kerux.com/) - has been printing biblical-theological material in the [Calvinistic](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Calvinism "wikilink") tradition since 1986
  - [Beginning With Moses: The Biblical Theology Briefings](https://web.archive.org/web/20071215142505/http://www.beginningwithmoses.org/) - articles, essays and book reviews by various scholars in biblical theology
  - [The Reemergence of Biblical Theology: What is Going On?](https://web.archive.org/web/20060210180025/http://catalystresources.org/issues/284motyer.html) from *Catalyst* ([United Methodist perspective](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:United_Methodist_Church "wikilink"))
  - [WWW Biblical Theology Index](http://www.bsw.org/index.php?l=41)
  - [Biblical and Systematic Theology: A Digest of Reformed Opinion on Their Proper Relationship](http://www.upper-register.com/papers/bt_st.html)
  - [*Monergism.com*](http://www.monergism.com/directory/link_category/Biblical-Theology/) - Directory of articles on Biblical Theology from the Reformed perspective.
  - [What is Biblical Theology?](http://www.hornes.org/theologia/rich-lusk/what-is-biblical-theology/) - an article by Rich Lusk on Biblical Theology.
