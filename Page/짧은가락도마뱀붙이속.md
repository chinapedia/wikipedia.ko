**짧은가락도마뱀붙이속**(*Stenodactylus*)는 **쇼트핑거 게코**(short-fingered geckos)라고 불리며, 도마뱀붙이류의 [중동](../Page/중동.md "wikilink"), [북아프리카](../Page/북아프리카.md "wikilink")에 [고유한](../Page/고유성.md "wikilink") [속이다](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/속_\(생물학\) "wikilink").\[1\]

## 분류

  - Iranian short-fingered gecko, *[Stenodactylus affinis](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stenodactylus_affinis "wikilink")*
  - Middle Eastern short-fingered gecko, *[Stenodactylus doriae](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stenodactylus_doriae "wikilink")*
  - Jordan short-fingered gecko, *[Stenodactylus grandiceps](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stenodactylus_grandiceps "wikilink")*
  - Southern short-fingered gecko, *[Stenodactylus leptocosymbotes](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stenodactylus_leptocosymbotes "wikilink")*
  - *[Stenodactylus mauritanicus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stenodactylus_mauritanicus "wikilink")*
  - Anderson's short-fingered gecko, *[Stenodactylus petrii](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/앤더슨짧은가락도마뱀붙이 "wikilink")*
  - Beautiful short-fingered gecko, *[Stenodactylus pulcher](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stenodactylus_pulcher "wikilink")*
  - Slevin's short-fingered gecko, *[Stenodactylus slevini](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stenodactylus_slevini "wikilink")*
  - *[Stenodactylus stenurus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stenodactylus_stenurus "wikilink")*
  - Lichtenstein's short-fingered gecko, *[Stenodactylus sthenodactylus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stenodactylus_sthenodactylus "wikilink")*
  - Yemen short-fingered gecko, *[Stenodactylus yemenensis](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stenodactylus_yemenensis "wikilink")*
