## 크레딧

  - Directed by 신재홍, 윤일상
  - Remix by 김윤성
  - Rap by 서태원, 김민기
  - Recorded & Mixed at Jigu Studio
  - Mastering : Sonic Korea
  - Music Video : 김세훈
  - Photography : 이재하
  - Design : 한주훈
  - Hair Styling, Make-Up & Coordinating : 이시언, Co,B
  - 판매량 : 330,000장
