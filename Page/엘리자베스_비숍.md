[섬네일](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/파일:Elizabeth_Bishop,_1934_yearbook_portrait.jpg "wikilink") **엘리자베스 비숍**(Elizabeth Bishop, [1911년](../Page/1911년.md "wikilink") [2월 8일](../Page/2월_8일.md "wikilink") \~ [1979년](../Page/1979년.md "wikilink") [10월 6일](../Page/10월_6일.md "wikilink"))은 [미국](../Page/미국.md "wikilink")의 [시인](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/시인 "wikilink")이자 [작가](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/작가 "wikilink")이다. 그녀는 1949년부터 1950년까지 [미국의 국민시인이였으며](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/미국의_국민시인 "wikilink"), 1956년 [퓰리처상](../Page/퓰리처상.md "wikilink")을 수상하였다. 또한 1970년 [내셔널 북 어워드](../Page/내셔널_북_어워드.md "wikilink"), 1976년 <span class="plainlinks">[노이슈타트 국제문학상](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neustadt_International_Prize_for_Literature)</span>을 수상하여 20세기를 대표하는 시인으로 꼽힌다.

## 수상내역

  - 1945: Houghton Mifflin Poetry Prize Fellowship
  - 1947: <span class="plainlinks">[Guggenheim Fellowship](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guggenheim_Fellowship) </span>
  - 1949: Appointed Consultant in Poetry at the Library of Congress
  - 1950: <span class="plainlinks">[American Academy of Arts and Letters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Academy_of_Arts_and_Letters) </span>Award
  - 1951: Lucy Martin Donelly Fellowship (awarded by Bryn Mawr College)
  - 1953: <span class="plainlinks">[Memrial Award Shelley Memorial Award](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shelley) </span>
  - 1954: Elected to lifetime membership in the <span class="plainlinks">[National Institute of Arts and Letters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Institute_of_Arts_and_Letters)</span>
  - 1956: <span class="plainlinks">[Pulitzer Prize for Poetry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pulitzer_Prize_for_Poetry)</span>
  - 1960: Chapelbrook Foundation Award
  - 1964: <span class="plainlinks">[Academy of American Poets Fellowship](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_winners_of_the_Academy_of_American_Poets'_Fellowship)</span>
  - 1968: Fellow of the <span class="plainlinks">[American Academy of Arts and Sciences](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Academy_of_Arts_and_Sciences)</span>\[1\]

  - 1969: The <span class="plainlinks">[Order of Rio Branco](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Order_of_Rio_Branco)</span> (awarded by the Brazilian government)
