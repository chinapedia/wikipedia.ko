## 수록곡

1.  "Star Sail" – 3:59
2.  "Slide Away" – 4:03
3.  "Already There" – 5:38
4.  "Beautiful Mind" – 5:27
5.  "The Sun, The Sea" – 5:16
6.  "Virtual World" – 6:20
7.  "Make It Till Monday" – 3:05
8.  "Blue" – 3:24
9.  "Butterfly" – 6:39
10. "See You in the Next One (Have a Good Time)" – 3:07
