[섬네일](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/파일:Benton_Chapel_Spire.jpg "wikilink") **밴더빌트 대학교 신학부**(Vanderbilt University Divinity School)는 미국 네쉬빌에 있는 밴더빌트 대학교에 있는 초교파 신학부이다. 1875년에 세워졌다.

## 유명 교수들

  - Langdon Brown Gilkey - [Professor](../Page/교수.md "wikilink") (1954–63)
  - Amy-Jill Levine - E. Rhodes and Leona B. Carpenter Professor of [New Testament](../Page/신약성경.md "wikilink") Studies
  - Wilhelm Pauck - Distinguished Professor of Church History (1967–72)
  - Jack M. Sasson - Mary Jane Werthan Professor of Jewish Studies and [Hebrew Bible](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/히브리어_성경 "wikilink")
  - Choon-Leong Seow
  - Kelly Miller Smith - former assistant dean
  - Eugene Sutton
  - Fernando Segovia - current president of the Society for Biblical Literature
  - [Paul C.H. Lim](../Page/임창하_\(신학자\).md "wikilink") - Associate Professor of the History of Christianity

## 유명 동문

  - Kenneth Lee Carder (D.Min. in 1980) - [United Methodist](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/연합_감리_교회 "wikilink") bishop
  - Earnest Sevier Cox (1880-1966), Methodist preacher, [white supremacist](../Page/백인_우월주의.md "wikilink")
  - [Al Gore](../Page/앨_고어.md "wikilink") - attended 1971-72 on a yearlong [Rockefeller Foundation](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/록펠러_재단 "wikilink") [scholarship](../Page/장학금.md "wikilink") for people planning secular careers
  - Clare Purcell (B.D. in 1916) - [Methodist](../Page/감리교.md "wikilink") [bishop](../Page/주교.md "wikilink")
  - Robert Hitchcock Spain (M.Div.) - United Methodist bishop
  - Amy Welborn (M.A. in Church History) - [Roman Catholic](../Page/로마_가톨릭교회.md "wikilink") [author](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/작가 "wikilink"), [columnist](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/칼럼니스트 "wikilink"), [activist](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/활동주의 "wikilink"), [academic](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/아카데미 "wikilink") and [public speaker](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/연설 "wikilink")
  - Don West - studied under Alva Taylor and Willard Uphaus
  - Shelli Yoder (M.Div.) - Miss Indiana 1992, former [Democratic Party](../Page/민주당_\(미국\).md "wikilink") nominee for U.S. Congress (IN-9)
