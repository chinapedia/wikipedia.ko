6.  [22](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/22 "wikilink")
7.  I Almost Do
8.  [We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/We_Are_Never_Ever_Getting_Back_Together "wikilink")

12. Sad Beautiful Tragic
13. The Lucky One
14. [Everything Has Changed](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Everything_Has_Changed "wikilink")
15. Starlight
16. [Begin Again](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Begin_Again "wikilink")
