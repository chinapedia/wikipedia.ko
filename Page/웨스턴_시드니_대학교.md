**웨스턴 시드니 대학교**(University of Western Sydney)는 [호주](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/호주 "wikilink") [시드니](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/시드니 "wikilink")에 위치한 공립 대학교이다.

## 학부

### 인문학부

  - 소통예술학과(School of Communication Arts)
  - 교육학과(School of Education)
  - 인류·언어학과(School of Humanities and Languages)
  - 심리학과(School of Psychology)
  - 사회과학과(School of Social Sciences)

### 사업학부

  - 회계학과(School of Accounting)
  - 경제·재정학과(School of Economics and Finance)
  - 법학과(School of Law)
  - 경영학과(School of Management)
  - 마케팅학과(School of Marketing)
  - Sydney Graduate School of Management

### 건강·과학부

  - 생물의학·건강과학과(School of Biomedical and Health Sciences)
  - 계산·수학과(School of Computing and Mathematics)
  - 공학과(School of Engineering)
  - 의학과(School of Medicine)
  - 자연과학과(School of Natural Sciences)
  - 간호·조산학과(School of Nursing and Midwifery)
