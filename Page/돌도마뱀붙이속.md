**돌도마뱀붙이속**(stone geckos, fat-tailed geckos)은 [호주](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/호주 "wikilink")에 서식하는 [도마뱀붙이류](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/도마뱀붙이류 "wikilink")의 일[속이다](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/속_\(생물학\) "wikilink"). 하위 [종들은](../Page/종_\(생물학\).md "wikilink") 형태적으로는 비슷하지만 유전적으로는 별개의 종이다.\[1\]

## 하위

돌도마뱀붙이속에는 후술할 27 종이 포함되어 있다:\[2\]

  - *[Diplodactylus ameyi](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_ameyi "wikilink")*  – eastern deserts fat-tailed gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus barraganae](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_barraganae "wikilink")*  – gulf fat-tailed gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus bilybara](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_bilybara "wikilink")*  – western fat-tailed gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus calcicolus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_calcicolus "wikilink")*  – south coast gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus capensis](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_capensis "wikilink")*  – [Cape Range](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cape_Range_National_Park "wikilink") stone gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus conspicillatus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_conspicillatus "wikilink")*  – burrow-plug gecko, variable fat-tailed gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus custos](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_custos "wikilink")*  – [Kimberley](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kimberley_\(Western_Australia\) "wikilink") fat-tailed gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus fulleri](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_fulleri "wikilink")*  – [Lake Disappointment](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Disappointment "wikilink") ground gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus furcosus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_furcosus "wikilink")*  – forked gecko, Ranges stone gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus galaxias](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_galaxias "wikilink")*  – Northern [Pilbara](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pilbara "wikilink") beak-faced gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus galeatus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_galeatus "wikilink")*  – helmeted gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus granariensis](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_granariensis "wikilink")*  – western stone gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus hillii](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_hillii "wikilink")*  – northern fat-tailed gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus kenneallyi](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_kenneallyi "wikilink")*  – Kenneally's gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus klugei](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_klugei "wikilink")*  – [Kluge's](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arnold_G._Kluge "wikilink") gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus laevis](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_laevis "wikilink")*  – desert fat-tailed gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus lateroides](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_lateroides "wikilink")*  – speckled stone gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus mitchelli](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_mitchelli "wikilink")*  – Pilbara stone gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus nebulosus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_nebulosus "wikilink")*
  - *[Diplodactylus ornatus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_ornatus "wikilink")*  – ornate stone gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus platyurus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_platyurus "wikilink")*  – eastern fat-tailed gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus polyophthalmus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_polyophthalmus "wikilink")*  – spotted sandplain gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus pulcher](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_pulcher "wikilink")*  – fine-faced gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus savagei](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_savagei "wikilink")*  – yellow-spotted Pilbara gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus tessellatus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_tessellatus "wikilink")*  – tessellated gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus vittatus](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_vittatus "wikilink")*  – eastern stone gecko, stone gecko, wood gecko
  - *[Diplodactylus wiru](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplodactylus_wiru "wikilink")*  – desert wood gecko

[주의사항](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/주의사항 "wikilink")([:en:Nota bene](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Nota_bene "wikilink")): 삽입어구의 [이명 명명자](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/이명법 "wikilink")([:en:Binominal nomenclature](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Binominal_nomenclature "wikilink"))는 원래 해당 종을 돌도마뱀붙이속(*Diplodactylus*)과는 다른 속에 속한 것으로 기술했을지도 모른다.
