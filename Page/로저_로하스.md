**Roger Fabricio Rojas Lazo** (born 9 June 1990), nicknamed *RoRo*, is a [Honduran](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Honduras "wikilink") professional [footballer](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/association_football "wikilink"). He currently plays for [Sabah](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sabah_FC "wikilink") in the [Azerbaijan Premier League](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/Azerbaijan_Premier_League "wikilink").
