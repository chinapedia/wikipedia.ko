**케네스 스콧 라터렛**(Kenneth Scott Latourette, 1884년 8월 6일- 1968년 12월 26일)는 [예일 대학교](../Page/예일_대학교.md "wikilink") 교수였으며, [중국](../Page/중국.md "wikilink"), [일본](../Page/일본.md "wikilink"), 그리고 세계 기독교에 대한 [미국](../Page/미국.md "wikilink")의 역사학자였다.

## 작품

  - *A history of the expansion of Christianity. [1.The first five centuries](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/iarchive:firstfivecenturi008505mbp "wikilink")* (1937)
  - *A history of the expansion of Christianity. [2.The thousand years of uncertainty. 500-1500](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/iarchive:historyoftheexpa008501mbp "wikilink").* (1938)
  - *A history of expansion of christianity. [3.Three centuries of advance: A.D. 1500-A.D. 1800](https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/iarchive:historyoftheexpa008493mbp "wikilink")* (1939)
  - *A history of expansion of Christianity. 4. The great century: in Europe and the United States of America; A.D. 1800-A.D. 1914* (1941)
  - *A history of the expansion of Christianity. 5. The great century in the Americas, Australasia, and Africa. 1800-1914* (1943)
  - *A history of the expansion of Christianity. 6, The great century in Northern Africa and Asia: A.D. 1800 - A.D. 1914* (1944)
  - *A history of expansion of Christianity. 7. Advance through storm: AD 1914 and after, with concluding generalizations* (1945)
